Request a grant

Metacloud Grants allow the DAO’s CLOUD to fund the creation of features or content for the Metacloud platform. Either individuals or teams may apply within one of five categories, and there are six levels of funding for each category. Before submitting your grant proposal, please review the Grants Framework here.

Aside from the requirements listed in the Grants Framework and Metacloud’s Content Policy, Terms of Use, and Code of Ethics, there are no constraints on the content or features that may be funded.
